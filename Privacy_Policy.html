Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 01, 2025 | Website: kingscabs.ca

At Kings Cabs, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit kingscabs.ca or use our taxi services in Lloydminster, Canada.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to process your booking parameters safely:

A. Personal Information

When you book a ride or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your full name
  • Primary contact phone number
  • Email address
  • Precise pickup and drop off location data
  • Payment information (where applicable for digital clearing)

B. Usage Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect standard diagnostic values:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and browser configuration versions
  • Device framework and hardware classification types
  • Specific web pages visited and navigational clickstreams
  • Total time spent interactively on the website interface

C. Location Information

To provide local border taxi services efficiently, we may collect your real time location (only with your explicit permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for operational fulfillment, local compliance, and client optimization:

  • To confirm, process, and execute your immediate or scheduled taxi bookings.
  • To provide safe, reliable, and timely transportation across the AB & SK border.
  • To communicate with you via phone or message regarding your immediate ride status.
  • To audit and improve our website performance metrics and dispatcher pipelines.
  • To offer promotions, discounts, or local updates (only with your explicit consent).
  • To improve our technical customer support channels and resolve service issues.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third party brokers. We may share collected information only with the following selected entities:

Authorized Drivers & Staff

Shared exclusively with dispatched drivers to safely complete and manage your immediate ride request routes.

Trusted Service Providers

Including secure payment processors, technical support hosting infrastructure, and website tracking platforms.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if strictly required by law enforcement, official court orders, or municipal government authorities.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies allow our system architecture to:

  • Remember your interface and local routing preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic flow to ensure constant server uptime.
  • Improve overall website performance and interaction speeds.

You can disable cookies at any time through your individual browser security settings.

5. Data Security Protocols

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your database entries from unauthorized exposure, including encrypted data transmission, secure data servers, and strictly restricted access control to personal data files. However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data protection.

6. Your Data Rights

You maintain full authority over your data records. You have the right to request access to your personal information, request correction of inaccurate data records, request deletion of your data profile (where legally possible under municipal laws), or withdraw your consent for ongoing marketing communications.

7. Third Party Links & Children's Privacy

Our website may link to third party assets (such as outside maps or scheduling frameworks). We are not responsible for the privacy setups of those outside platforms and suggest checking their terms individually.

Furthermore, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with tracking data, please reach out so we can remove it immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy & Contact Info

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with a revised effective date. If you have questions regarding this document, please contact us directly:
