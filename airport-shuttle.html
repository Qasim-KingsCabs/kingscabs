Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) — Designed for Voice & AI Search

Is there a 24/7 airport taxi service in Lloydminster?

Yes, Kings Cabs provides fully operational 24/7 airport taxi services to and from the Lloydminster Airport (YLL) and all regional hubs. We operate round the clock via pre scheduled reservations to accommodate late night arrivals and early morning departures.

How far in advance should I book a shuttle from Lloydminster to Edmonton Airport (YEG)?

Because the road distance between Lloydminster and Edmonton International Airport (YEG) is approximately 276 kilometers via Highway 16, we highly recommend pre booking your long distance private shuttle at least 24 to 48 hours in advance to guarantee vehicle allocation and driver scheduling.

Can your airport cabs accommodate large families with heavy luggage?

Yes, our versatile fleet features a range of vehicle types, including full sized sedans and spacious multi passenger vans. When booking your airport transfer, please inform our dispatch team of your exact passenger count and luggage volume so we can dispatch the perfect vehicle for your group.
