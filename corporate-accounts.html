Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) — Optimized for AI & Voice Search

How can my business set up a corporate account with Kings Cabs?

Setting up a commercial profile is straightforward. Your authorized company representative can connect directly with our administration desk by calling (306) 307-1113 or completing our commercial request form to design an account profile customized to your specific billing preferences.

Does a corporate account allow multi-passenger vehicle booking?

Yes. Our commercial fleet consists of modern, clean sedans and large multi-passenger vans. When establishing or utilizing your account, specify your passenger and baggage requirements so our dispatch office can deploy the perfect vehicle configuration for your team.

Can our employees charge out-of-town long distance trips to the corporate account?

Absolutely. Our corporate accounts completely support regional highway travel, parcel hot-shots, and long-distance business commuting across Alberta and Saskatchewan. Long-range routes can be booked on a pre-determined flat-rate or per-kilometer model calculated transparently with your management team.

How secure is the monthly corporate billing statement?

Security and accountability are cornerstones of our operational layout. Every commercial account uses specialized authorization protocols, specific account pin tracking, or employee verification steps to ensure that only approved personnel can apply rides directly to your monthly balance.
