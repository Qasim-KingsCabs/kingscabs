How Our Drive Home Service Operates

Our process is highly coordinated to ensure maximum safety and speed. When you book a Drive Home team, we deploy a vehicle tracking team to your immediate location:

Step Sequence How It Works What To Expect
1. Request & Location Call dispatch and provide your current pickup location and your destination. A rapid arrival window is quoted directly by our border-city team.
2. Two-Driver Dispatch A Kings Cabs vehicle arrives with two of our professional staff members. One driver takes your keys to drive your car, while the other follows safely behind.
3. Safe Return Home You ride comfortably inside your own car while our driver navigates local streets. Your vehicle is parked exactly where you request, and your keys are returned to you.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can your staff drive manual (stick-shift) vehicles?

Yes. When you call our dispatch office, simply mention if your vehicle is a manual transmission. We will instantly route a skilled professional driver who is fully experienced with manual gearboxes to your location.

Is this service available on both sides of the border?

Absolutely. We provide complete bi-provincial coverage. Whether your car is parked at a venue on the Alberta side or a residential community on the Saskatchewan side, our teams navigate both sectors flawlessly.

Are your drivers insured to operate my vehicle?

Yes. Kings Cabs works with certified, fully screened drivers to ensure that your car is handled with the highest level of professionalism and care throughout the entire trip.

Can I book this service for a family member or friend?

Yes. You can call and arrange a safe drive home for anyone. Simply provide our dispatch team with the vehicle's location, the color/make of the car, and the contact number of the person with the keys.
