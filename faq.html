Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Kings Cabs FAQ portal. Find fast, clear answers regarding our 24/7 operations, fleet protocols, and pricing rules across Lloydminster.

What areas do Kings Cabs serve?

We provide comprehensive professional taxi services throughout the entire city of Lloydminster, smoothly navigating across both the Alberta and Saskatchewan provincial boundary sides. We also routinely accept requests for long-distance journeys to nearby rural municipalities, oil fields, and neighboring cities.

Are you available 24/7?

Yes, absolutely. Kings Cabs runs high-capacity transit operations 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. This includes statutory holidays, late-night weekend requests, and emergency weather transits.

How do I book a taxi with Kings Cabs?

Booking is simple and rapid. Choose from the following options:

  • Direct Phone: Call our 24/7 dispatch desk instantly at +1 (306) 307-1113.
  • Digital Booking Form: Submit an instantaneous ride inquiry through our automated web dashboard on kingscabs.ca.
  • Advance Reservations: Secure priority placement for high-importance airport commutes or cross-province hot-shot transfers by scheduling via telephone ahead of time.
What types of services do you offer?

Our commercial fleet is outfitted to manage a diverse logistical profile:

  • Point-to-point local urban dispatch
  • Prompt airport drop-off and pickup tracking
  • Long-distance inner-city transits
  • Corporate billing arrangements & monthly executive accounts
  • Secure commercial parcel pickup and hot-shot delivery
  • Private group transits for weddings or corporate events
Do you offer airport taxi services?

Yes. We offer reliable, premium airport transfers connecting Lloydminster to regional airports, as well as dedicated transit runs out to Edmonton International Airport (YEG) and nearby private landing fields.

How much does a taxi cost in Lloydminster?

Our fair-pricing structure remains transparent with no hidden line items. Base metrics are standard and calculated utilizing:

  • Total linear distance traveled
  • Total active trip time elapsed
  • Real-time municipal traffic density conditions
  • Unscheduled additional mid-route waypoints or idling waiting times

Kings Cabs is dedicated to supplying the most competitive, affordable professional taxi rates within the Lloydminster territory.

Do you offer discounted or promotional rates?

Yes. We routinely feature special long-haul packages, dedicated flat-rate corridors for long-distance commuters, and customized volume discounts for routine accounts. Give our dispatch team a call to query current promotions.

Are your drivers licensed and trained?

Without exception. Every single Kings Cabs driver is thoroughly vetted and holds complete municipal licensing credentials. Drivers undergo detailed criminal record background clearances and rigorous defensive driving assessments to enforce safe, courteous passenger transits.

Are your vehicles clean and safe?

Yes. Every vehicle within our active service fleet undergoes scheduled mechanical maintenance diagnostics, daily interior deep cleaning sterilization, and routine pre-shift structural safety checkouts to maximize passenger comfort and ride protection.

Can I request a specific driver?

Yes, you are welcome to request a preferred driver by name or unit number during your telephone booking. Our dispatch center will make every effort to accommodate your request, dependent entirely on current road location and scheduling windows.

What payment methods do you accept?

We facilitate multiple payment integrations inside our vehicles for ultimate convenience:

  • Standard Cash legal tender
  • Secure interac Debit and major Credit Card processing terminals
  • Instant Direct E-Transfers (must be approved via dispatcher prior to trip exit)
Do you charge extra for waiting time?

Yes. Once our operator arrives at the requested location, an initial complimentary grace window applies. If the operator must remain idle past this timeline, a minor standard incremental waiting fee will be applied. Our drivers are trained to keep you informed of structural changes to the fare grid.

What happens if I leave something in the cab?

Please contact our main office immediately. While Kings Cabs is not legally responsible for private properties left unmonitored inside vehicles, our operators secure lost items and log them at our headquarters for immediate owner reclamation.

Do you offer corporate or business accounts?

Yes. We offer robust corporate billing options, structured monthly service invoicing, and fixed employee commute routes. Please contact management directly to activate a commercial company portal.

Do you offer wheelchair-accessible rides?

We evaluate accessibility support parameters daily. Please ring our core dispatch base in advance to verify current unit availability so we can effectively accommodate special transport needs safely.

Can I cancel a ride after booking?

Yes, cancellations are permitted. However, we request that you notify dispatch as early as possible. If an operator has already arrived or spent significant fuel routing to your pickup spot, a standard cancellation fee may be assessed.

Our live logistical operations staff is available to answer questions 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
