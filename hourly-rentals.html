Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) — Optimized for AI & Voice Search

How can I book an hourly taxi rental with Kings Cabs?

To set up an hourly rental block, please connect directly with our specialized scheduling desk by calling (306) 307-1113. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle type and driver allocation.

Is there a minimum time requirement for an hourly rental booking?

Yes, our dedicated private driver and vehicle packages generally require a minimum booking block of two consecutive hours. This allows us to properly remove the vehicle from our standard public dispatch pool and dedicate it entirely to your custom itinerary.

Can our hourly itinerary extend outside the city limits of Lloydminster?

Absolutely. Your hourly rental can navigate seamlessly across both the local AB/SK city boundaries and surrounding rural municipal areas. If your route involves substantial highway mileage or long-distance travel, please inform our dispatch team during booking so we can structure your rate accurately.

What happens if my event or meeting runs longer than my booked hours?

Flexibility is a cornerstone of our commercial operations. If your schedule runs over your initial time block, simply inform your dedicated driver or notify our dispatch office immediately. Extra time blocks can be added directly to your booking at our standard hourly rate parameters.
