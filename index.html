Who We Are

Lloydminster’s Premier 24/7 Professional Taxi & Transit Network

For over a decade, Kings Cabs has stood as the gold standard for safe, legal, and highly regulated transportation across the unique bi-provincial layout of the Border City. Operating round-the-clock, 365 days a year, we seamlessly bridge the Alberta and Saskatchewan statelines, ensuring that local residents, corporate teams, and visitors navigate Lloydminster without logistical delays.

We pride ourselves on more than just getting you from point A to point B. Whether managing immediate local city rides, coordinating essential municipal senior program vouchers, or facilitating smooth long-distance airport transfers along the Yellowhead Corridor to Edmonton, our mission remains centered on professional integrity, pristine fleet standards, and absolute punctuality.
