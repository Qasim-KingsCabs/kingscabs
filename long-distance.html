Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I book a long distance taxi from Lloydminster to a neighboring town?

Yes, absolutely. Kings Cabs provides full service, 24/7 out of town transportation from Lloydminster to all neighboring rural communities, including Kitscoty, Vermilion, Marshall, Lashburn, and beyond. We recommend pre booking these trips to ensure perfect timing.

How are long distance taxi rates calculated?

Unlike short city rides that rely on standard distance meters, our long distance and regional trips are calculated using highly competitive, pre determined flat rates or per kilometer regional structures. This guarantees you know the exact cost of your trip before your vehicle ever arrives.

What happens if bad winter weather impacts my regional booking?

Safety is our absolute priority. While our vehicles are heavily optimized for severe winter highway driving, our dispatch team actively monitors regional road reports and highway closures. If a route becomes dangerous or closed by provincial authorities, we will work with you instantly to safely reschedule your travel window.

Do you offer long distance private shuttle services for large groups?

Yes. Our fleet contains spacious multi passenger vans perfect for families, industrial work crews, corporate groups, or event parties traveling long distances together. Please specify your exact passenger count and luggage requirements when booking so we can deploy the perfect vehicle.
