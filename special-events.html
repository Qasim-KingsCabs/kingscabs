Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) — Optimized for AI & Voice Search

How far in advance should I book group transportation for a special event?

For large-scale gatherings like weddings, corporate banquets, or major community festivals, we highly recommend securing your vehicles at least 2 to 4 weeks in advance. This ensures guaranteed vehicle availability, dedicated driver scheduling, and pristine fleet allocation for your dates.

Can your event vehicles accommodate large groups traveling together?

Yes, absolutely. Our versatile fleet features spacious, modern multi-passenger vans perfectly suited to transport bridal parties, corporate groups, and families together. Please specify your exact passenger numbers to our dispatch team when booking so we can deploy the ideal vehicle mix.

Do you offer flat-rate options for evening-long wedding guest shuttles?

Yes. To make commercial budgeting as simple as possible for event hosts, we offer competitive, pre-determined group pricing models and specialized hourly block structures tailored directly to your custom itinerary. Connect with our reservations office to discuss a quote for your event coordinates.

What happens if our corporate event or gala runs later than initially planned?

Operational flexibility is a foundational priority at Kings Cabs. If your event schedules shift or run past your expected end-time, simply notify our 24/7 dispatch office immediately. We maintain round-the-clock staffing to adjust driver allocations dynamically to match your live timeline.
