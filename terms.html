A. Transparent Pricing

Kings Cabs offers affordable and highly competitive taxi rates. Fares depend natively on distance traveled, time elapsed, traffic conditions, special service requests, and total waiting time accumulated.

B. Payment Methods

Accepted payment options include Cash, Debit/Credit (if terminal is available inside the vehicle), or E-transfer (if offered and pre-cleared by dispatch controls).

Extra fees may apply for excess waiting times, additional unscheduled stops, road tolls, parking fees, or cleaning fees (ranging up to full professional detailing costs for spills, vomiting, or physical damage).

Passengers must wear seat belts at all times under Canadian law, treat the driver and vehicle with respect, and avoid smoking, drinking alcohol, or using illegal substances inside the vehicle. Any intentional or accidental damage to the taxi vehicle will result in direct repair charges.
